The E2Coach team was selected as one of five winners of the 2012 UM Provost's Teaching Innovation Prize for their submission: "Better than Expected: Using Tailored Communication to Optimize Learning". You can read more about this award and the other winners here.

Horizon Report 2012

February 2012

Science, Technology, Mathematics, and Engineering (STEM). The University of Michigan uses a system called E2Coach in large introductory STEM courses. E2Coach uses information about student background, motivations, and recent performance to provide feedback, encouragement, and advice that are tailored to each student.
